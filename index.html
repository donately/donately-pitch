Feature Details

Embedded Forms

Beautifully designed and customizable donation forms that embed in 5min or less.

Campaign Pages

Easily customizable campaign pages designed to motivate giving. They link to your site, and make donors think they never left.

Peer Fundraising

Empower your supporters to become active fundraisers with customizable peer-to-peer campaign pages.

Customizable Reports

Own your data with simple reporting. It allows you to know your donors better and improve your fundraising efforts by catering to what motivates your donors to give.

Fundraising Insights

Assign account admins, and give your fundraisers access to their metrics and yours.
